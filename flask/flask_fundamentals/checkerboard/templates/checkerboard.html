Customization Instructions:

If you would like to personalize the checkerboard...

  • http://localhost:5000 - will display 8 by 8 checkerboard
  • http://localhost:5000/x - will display 8 by x checkerboard
  • http://localhost:5000/x/y - will display x by y checkerboard. For example, http://localhost:5000/10/10 will display a 10 by 10 checkerboard.
  • http://localhost:5000/x/y/color1/color2 will display x by y checkerboard, with the respective chosen colors.
